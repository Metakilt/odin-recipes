Lasagna Recipe

Description

John Chandler submitted this lasagna and it became the world's best lasagna, I'm not even kidding

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatos for authenticity
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Cover with foil and bake in an oven
  6. Let it rest
