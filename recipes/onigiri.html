Onigiri(Japanese Rice Balls)

Description

This is a traditional dish in Japan that is easy to make for lunchboxes or bento meals

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of uncooked white rice
  • water
  • salt
  • nori sheets
  • bonito shavings
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice
  2. combine 1 cup water with salt in a small bowl and dampen hands with it
  3. Divide rice to 8 portions
  4. Shape it into a triangle and wrap with nori sheets and sprinkle with sesame seeds
  5. Add Seafood inside if you want
